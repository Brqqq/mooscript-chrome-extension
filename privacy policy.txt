Privacy Policy
Last updated: December 12, 2021

This privacy policy describes how your data is used. If you are reading this, then: hi Google review employee, you're probably the only one who will ever read this.

Mooscript stores everything you filled out in the forms in Mooscript, such as email addresses and passwords. This data is stored locally on your device. 

Mooscript uses this data when it tries to authenticate you with Mobstar.cc over an HTTPS connection. This is expected; the purpose of Mooscript is to automatically access mobstar.cc using your account information. This data is not stored or transmitted to anywhere else.

Mooscript does not gather any data in the background. No telemetry or other usage information is gathered. 

For questions, please contact cow@mooscript.com
